MASTERS RESEARCH: THE APPROACHING CURVE
========================================

THESIS TITLE:
"The Approaching Curve"

RESEARCH QUESTION:
"How does the privatization of digital infrastructural development affect citizen living and quality of life?"

FOCUS:
AMAN explores how the shift in power and decision-making from public to private sectors regarding digital infrastructure impacts social responsibility and welfare maintenance.

MAIN FINDINGS:
- Privatization's effects vary significantly based on local consumer populations and government structures
- In developing countries, privatization provides necessary commerce structure and regulation
- Citizen-actor dialogue about local infrastructure is crucial for maintaining social equity
