MOMO
To capture movements of people through the creation of precious moments.In partnership with the Paris IDF regional council, and the audience of Mme Valerie Pecresse, we took on the challenge of renewing the urban news kiosk, for the suburban region.
​
Innovating on the classical Parisian newsstands, we began with the question “How can we foster a unique, local, social spot that connects citizen and information flows?” we conducted informal interviews with consumers and kiosk attendants to understand the stakes and challenges of this redesign, before beginning conception.
