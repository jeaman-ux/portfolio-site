top of pageWorkAbout Me + XPThe Urban InterfaceThe urban interface is an exercise about analyzing the urban landscape as a collection of actionable interfaces, much like interactions in the digital sphere. I created this mapping to represent the way in which people interact physically, visually, and emotionally with this space that links a river-side footpath, highway, and public park.
​
I conducted citizen-user interviews on the street surrounding the park, to better understand the way in which people interact with the space, and how it could be improved.
​
The aim of this brief study became about finding leverage points in users' feelings of immersion in manmade and/or natural environments Back
