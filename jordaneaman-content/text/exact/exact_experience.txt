top of pageWorkAbout Me + XP About Me Hello, I'm JordanWelcome to my portfolio site. I am an American designer living and working in Paris, France since 2015.
​
I am passionate about UX design in all its applications : cities, web, services, applications, and research & analysis. Primarily, I consider myself a service designer, and use service design methods and tools to build a systemic analysis and approach to each of my projects.
I am highly motivated by the “user/citizen-centric” future of design, as it extends far beyond conventional applications (services, experiences, products, etc.) like politics and management. I believe that design is, at its roots, a powerful tool that lays the groundwork for innovation and progress, and that there has never been a more important time for designers to reach and extend any limits possible.
​
I hope you enjoy discovering my work. Don't hesitate to get in touch! CV
