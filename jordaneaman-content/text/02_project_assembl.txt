ASSEMBL BY BLUENOVE
===================

PROJECT TITLE:
Assembl - A collective intelligence platform supporting community discussion and idea prioritization

DESCRIPTION:
The platform facilitates "six native applications for steering collective intelligence initiatives" where "communities discuss, debate, and prioritise ideas." It serves both public/private sector clients and functions as the primary analysis tool for consultants.

TIMELINE & ROLE:
2019-2021
UX, UI, service design, product design, prototyping, user research, testing, product ownership

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES:
- Piloted all design activities at Bluenove Paris (October 2019 - May 2020)
- Delivered MVP of two new platform modules
- Implemented industrialized CMS
- Redesigned entire back-office
- Served as design lead and scrum product owner simultaneously

PROCESS & METHODOLOGY:

Service Design:
Separated existing platform into "mono-functional applications to better understand how to improve the participant experience," emphasizing communication optimization between participants and communities.

Design System:
Built modular Assembl Design System spanning "Figma and Storybook" to support open-source vision and enable communities to develop custom modules.

VISUAL ASSETS PRESENT:
- Service blueprint diagram
- Inscription/registration flow wireframe
- Design system components (Figma/Storybook examples)
- Five interface screenshots showing moderation, back-office, and metrics dashboards
- Tool icons (Figma, Notion, Jira, WordPress, Elementor, Crowdin, Medium)

OUTCOME:
Delivered comprehensive design specifications and prototypes for subsequent implementation across development teams over one year.
