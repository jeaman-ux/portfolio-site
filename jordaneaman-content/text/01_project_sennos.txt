SENNOS AT SENNDER
==================

PROJECT TITLE:
SennOS - Supporting Sennder's shippers, carriers and operators with modern freight logistics software

TIMELINE & ROLE:
2021 - present
UX, UI, service design, product design, prototyping, user research, user testing, product strategy

PROJECT OVERVIEW:
Work on improving shipper and carrier experiences across Sennder's main software platforms, with particular focus on carrier shipment marketplace services and shipper tracking tools.

SHIPPERS (OTTERS SOFTWARE):
- Tool for SMBs to program and track shipments with simplified order creation
- Enterprise clients access bulk order creation and shipping analytics
- Focus: reducing complexity, providing visibility and clarity, creating self-explanatory interactions

Notable Projects:
- Notification system UX/UI
- Dashboard research and redesign
- Tracking system and interface research
- Technical spec creation

CARRIERS (ORCAS SOFTWARE):
- Platform for carriers to bid, book shipments, and plan/dispatch orders
- Supports marketplace experience of brokerage services
- Approach uses service design methods: system mapping, service blueprints, customer experience flows, interface interactions
- Emphasis on cross-functional partnership with engineering and product management

VISUAL ELEMENTS:
- Dashboard interfaces and notification systems
- Tracking interface designs
- Marketplace order display and viewing features
