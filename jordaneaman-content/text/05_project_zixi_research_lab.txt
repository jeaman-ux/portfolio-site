ZIXI RESEARCH LAB
==================

YEAR & DISCIPLINES:
2018 | UX and service design

PROJECT OVERVIEW:
This project addressed rural depopulation in Zixi County, Jiangxi, China. The initiative aimed to tackle migration through economic opportunity and educational development.

KEY PROBLEM:
Rural residents were leaving villages due to "lack of reliable economic opportunity, proper facilities for living and working, and well formed educational systems."

SOLUTION APPROACH:
The team proposed the Zixi Qinliangshan Research Lab, a public-private partnership model combining domestic and foreign universities with local private companies to establish research facilities in forest park villages.

IMPLEMENTATION STRATEGY:
- Research focus areas: environmental studies, engineering, ecology, biology, biochemistry, and cultural studies
- Revenue model: Academic partnerships fund research labs while generating income for villages
- Long-term benefit: Funding supports local school development to encourage family returns

RESEARCH METHODS:
The designers conducted week-long field research across 7 villages, including interviews with farmers and local officials with translator assistance to understand community needs and daily life.

OUTCOMES:
The team presented detailed short and long-term development plans to state officials and residents, maintaining ongoing contact to support future regional progress initiatives.
