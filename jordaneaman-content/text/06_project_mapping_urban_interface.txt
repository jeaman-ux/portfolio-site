URBAN INTERFACE: MAPPING URBAN INTERACTIONS
=============================================

PROJECT OVERVIEW:
The project presents a design analysis of "the urban landscape as a collection of actionable interfaces, much like interactions in the digital sphere."

DESCRIPTION:
The designer created a mapping to represent how people physically, visually, and emotionally interact with a space connecting a riverside footpath, highway, and public park. The work involved "citizen-user interviews on the street surrounding the park, to better understand the way in which people interact with the space."

RESEARCH FOCUS:
The study aimed at discovering "leverage points in users' feelings of immersion in manmade and/or natural environments." This suggests an exploration of how environmental design elements influence human experience and engagement with public spaces.

VISUAL ASSETS:
The project includes three documentary photographs alongside an interface map, documenting the physical and spatial analysis undertaken during the research phase.
