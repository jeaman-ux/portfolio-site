MOVINON HUB - MICHELIN INNOVATION LAB
======================================

CLIENT:
Michelin

ROLE:
Freelance design consultant

PROJECT OVERVIEW:
Jordan Eaman helped conceptualize "a 3-part innovation ecosystem in which they could re-establish themselves as mobility innovators and experts."

SCOPE & TIMELINE:
Over four months, the team developed a proposal to transform Michelin's decommissioned manufacturing facility in Clermont-Ferrand, France.

CORE CONCEPT:
The project centered on ArtScience—"the collaboration of science and engineering with design and creativity"—as a leverage point for Michelin's pivot toward mobility innovation consulting.

DELIVERABLES:
- Service and brand design
- Interactive experiences
- Spatial design for events ranging from immersive exploration sessions to conferences on data collection in modern mobility systems
- A-B testing methodology to validate proposals across internal teams
- Detailed customer journey documentation for B2B offerings

DESIGN FEATURES:
Graphics for promotional materials describing the ArtScience Studio and CoDesign Station, plus redacted plans for a two-day immersive exploration session.

STATUS:
The project remains ongoing as of the portfolio update.
