X-TRACT
=======

PROJECT TITLE:
X-Tract

TAGLINE:
"Connecting skiers and first responders"

YEAR & DISCIPLINES:
2019 | UX, UI, product design, prototyping

DURATION:
Month-long mission

CLIENT:
X-Fabric

OBJECTIVE:
Create first demo prototype for X-Tract, a first-aid and response application

PROJECT DESCRIPTION:
The designer worked with the X-Fabric team across 5 week-long sprints to rapidly prototype both the mobile app and operator back-office.

PROCESS:
1. Product benchmark and audit of existing graphic content
2. Wireframing (sketched collaboratively in an afternoon)
3. Creating graphic chart and V1 user interface
4. Iterative refinements over 2 weeks
5. Final product pitch and user testing plan

VISUAL ASSETS PRESENT:
- A desktop interface mockup (600×314px)
- Mobile app design (600×450px)
- A flyer or promotional material (600×400px)
