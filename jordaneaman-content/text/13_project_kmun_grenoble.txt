KMUN GRENOBLE
==============

PROJECT TITLE:
KmUn Grenoble - Mobility incentive project

LOCATION:
Grenoble, France

PROJECT OVERVIEW:
KmUn encourages sustainable commuting in Grenoble by rewarding eco-responsible travel choices. Users earn credits for walking, biking, carpooling, and using public transit—credits redeemable for services or local currency.

CORE CONCEPT:
The system creates a virtuous cycle: "the more the user moves in an eco-responsible way, the more the community invests in the dedicated infrastructures."

KEY FEATURES:
- Differentiated kilometer values based on transportation mode (pedestrian travel worth more than tram, which exceeds carpooled distance)
- Commitment-based approach preventing penalties for shorter daily commutes
- Partnership development with CEA and Orange Telecom
- Multi-demographic consideration accounting for urban, suburban, and rural commuters

DELIVERABLES:
The final output was a magazine explaining the concept, distributed within the partner company to drive awareness and adoption in the territory.

PROJECT EMPHASIS:
The design emphasizes sustainability, durability, and long-term behavioral change within the local mobility ecosystem.
