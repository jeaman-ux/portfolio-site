ASSISE DU DESIGN
=================

PROJECT TITLE:
Assise du Design

DESCRIPTION:
"A national organisation for the promotion, advancement and sharing of Design and its many applications."

BACKGROUND:
This initiative was launched in April 2019 by France's Ministry of Economy and Finance and Ministry of Culture. A national conference in December 2019 produced recommendations linking design to innovation as a tool for solving economic and social challenges.

JORDAN'S INVOLVEMENT:
"While working as a Service Designer at 40/60 Design Studio I led our team in reflexion and delivery of our own set of recommendations."

PROJECT TYPE:
Design strategy and national policy recommendations

CLIENT:
France's Ministry of Economy and Finance and Ministry of Culture (through 40/60 Design Studio)
